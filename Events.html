Exciting Events in Bali

Discover the vibrant culture of Bali through these exciting local events!

Bali Arts Festival

Date: June - July

The Bali Arts Festival is a month-long celebration of traditional and modern arts. Expect vibrant parades, performances, and exhibitions showcasing the best of Bali's art and culture.

Nyepi (Day of Silence)

Date: March

Experience Bali's Day of Silence where the entire island shuts down in reverence. It is a unique opportunity for meditation and reflection. The day before, thereâ€™s a vibrant parade known as Ogoh-Ogoh!

Ubud Writers & Readers Festival

Date: October

Celebrate literature and the arts with international and local writers, readers, artists, and thought leaders in the charming town of Ubud.

Galungan Festival

Date: Twice a year (every 210 days)

Galungan marks the time when ancestral spirits visit Earth. Witness beautifully decorated streets and experience temple ceremonies in this important Balinese holiday.
